# Composer vs. AI Interpretation
## Cinematic Emotion Lab · EXP-004

> *"The machine hears amplitude, frequency, and time. The composer hears grief."*

The Composer Gap is the structured divergence between what an acoustic model predicts and what a film composer says they constructed. It is not a failure condition - it is the most important finding of this research program.

## Working Hypotheses

- The model collapses emotional ambiguity onto high-confidence categorical neighbors
- Dynamic intensity is conflated with emotional weight
- Cues built on negative space - withheld resolution, deliberate silence - are systematically underscored
- Model accuracy degrades when composers deliberately subvert tempo-mode expectations

## Study Design

Track A - composer annotation: intent, intensity, narrative function, harmonic strategy, free-text reasoning.
Track B - model prediction: acoustic feature input only.
Comparison: where do tracks agree, diverge, and why?
