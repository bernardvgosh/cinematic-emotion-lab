# Cinematic Emotion Lab

Can AI understand why a film score creates suspense, grief, triumph, or emotional tension?

This research initiative explores the intersection of:

* Film composition
* Music cognition
* Machine learning
* Emotional analysis
* Narrative structure
* Computational storytelling

The project combines feature extraction, composer annotation, and AI-based emotion modeling to study how cinematic music shapes human perception.

Research areas currently being explored:

* Tempo vs emotional intensity
* Harmonic movement and narrative tension
* Spectral density and suspense
* AI classification of cinematic emotion
* Human composer interpretation vs model prediction
* Architecture-inspired approaches to musical space and emotional pacing

Current Status:

* Dataset construction underway
* Annotation framework in progress
* Baseline ML experiments beginning soon
