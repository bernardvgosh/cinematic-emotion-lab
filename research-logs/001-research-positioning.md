**Date:** 2026-06-06
**Status:** Active

## Positioning Statement

Cinematic Emotion Lab is an independent AI research initiative studying the computational architecture of emotional perception in film music. It operates at the intersection of music information retrieval, affective computing, film theory, and machine learning.

The research is positioned as:
- Empirically rigorous: every claim tested against annotated data
- Theoretically grounded: research questions derive from musicology, cognitive science, and narrative theory
- Compositionally informed: ground truth is composer intent, not listener response
- Honest about limits: the Composer Gap is a primary finding, not a limitation to minimize

## What Makes This Distinct

Most music emotion research operates on popular music, with listener perception as ground truth, using valence-arousal as the target space. This research operates on cinematic music, with composer intent as ground truth, using a richer emotion taxonomy tuned to narrative function.

The distinction is not cosmetic. It changes what can be measured, what counts as accuracy, and what the interesting failures look like.

## Research Identity

> Film music is a precision emotional transmission system. This lab is building the instruments to decode it.
